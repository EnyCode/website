# Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 30th August 2023

Thanks for visiting enymc.dev. I highly value your privacy and am commited to protecting your info.
This privacy policy outlines how we use your data.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information

When you subscribe to my newsletter, I collect the following personal information:

- Your **email address**: this is the email address my newsletter is sent to.

### 1.2 Usage Data

I also collect non-personal analytical data when you use our website using Cloudflare Web Analytics.

- Operating System
- Browser
- Device Type (e.g. Desktop or Mobile
- Referring Website
- How many times you visit
- What pages you visit

This data is completely anonomized and is not linked together.

## 2. How I use your information

Your data is used for the following:

### 2.1 Newsletter Subscriptions

We use your email to send you my newsletter.

### 2.2 Website Improvement

We use our analytical data to see what pages could be improved and what articles perform the best to create the content that you want to see.

## 3. Data Security

I take data security seriously and have implemented reasonable measures to protect your information. However, please be aware that no method of data transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

## 4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent or share your email address with any third parties. Your email address is shared with MailJet, who delivers our newsletter to your mailbox, who are obligated to not share your email address to third parties.

## 5. Your choices

You have the following rights regarding your information, and they can be used at any time via email at [eny@enymc.dev](mailto:eny@enymc.dev)

### 5.1 Access

You can request access to your personal data. This will be provided within a reasonable time frame (maximium 30 days)

### 5.2 Rectification

If you believe your personal data is incorrect, it can be rectified either by requesting via email or using our subscription management tool linked at the bottom of our newsletter to change your email address.

### 5.3 Deletion

You can request the deletion of your data at any time, either by email or by unsubscribing from our newsletter, where the data will then immediately be removed from my systems and removed from our third parties servers.

### 5.4 Opt Out

You can opt out of recieving the newsletter at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link at the bottom of any newsletter you recieve.

## 6. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically to reflect changes in my practises. Any changes will posted on this page, and an article will be released detailing the changes that occurred.

## 7. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, either open a [GitHub issue](https://github.com/EnyCode/enymc.dev/issues) or for more private matters, email me directly at [eny@enymc.dev](mailto:eny@enymc.dev)
